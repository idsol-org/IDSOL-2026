The Olympiad

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL 2026) is a design competition where students apply first principles to propose practical use cases for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI).

Mission & Vision

The Problem

Traditional education often fails to test the complexities of real-world data application and architectural resilience.

The Solution

IDSOL 2026 provides a global stage for Applied Intelligence, where engineering rigor meets creative problem-solving.

The Goal

Bridging the gap between academic theory and enterprise-ready solutions through standardized benchmarking.

Competition Categories

INFRA

Enterprise-oriented Infrastructure

Designed for large-scale integration within for-profit and non-profit companies. Focuses on systemic resilience and scalability.

DEV

Consumer-oriented Products

Designed for individual utility, focusing on leisure, hobbies, or semi-professional interests.
